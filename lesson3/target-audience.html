Target Audience:

A Little Weathered is targeted towards working-class adults and their spouses. In this area, they are most likely to be white, married, have a high school diploma, have a couple of kids, and work in the phosphate industry, making weed-killer or fertilizer. The majority of these workers drive 15-25 minutes to get to work, depending on how rural their area is.

Personas:

Jacob Smith

Jacob is a 30-year-old blue-collar worker. He lives in Soda Springs, Idaho, and works 15 minutes away as a phosphate miner in the Conda Mine run by Itafos. Jacob has been at the mine since graduating from high school. He now works as a Surveyor for the site. Jacob checks the weather every single day as he prepares for his commute to work. Weather always has to be factored-in while working at an open mine site.

Senarios:

  • What are the road conditions like on my way to work?
  • Will vision be impeded on the job site?
  • Is the upcoming storm bad enough to halt production?

Hannah Johnson

Hannah is a 33-year-old full-time soccer mom. She lives in Fish Haven, Idaho, with her husband and two kids. She runs an Etsy store home business when she can and runs her kids to various activities the rest of the time. Hannah stays on top of the weather to make sure the softball game is still happening on Friday night and to see if the kids can go fishing with their dad over the weekend so she can get some work done.

Senarios:

  • How will the weather affect the kids' activities?
  • Is it good fishing weather this weekend?
  • How close was the earthquake this morning to Grandma?
